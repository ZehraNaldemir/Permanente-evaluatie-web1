Who am I?

I am Zehra Naldemir and my interest in fitness comes from my younger years.
At the age of 10 I started swimming, I can say that after fitness it is one of my favorite sports.
When I was 16 I started playing volleyball. In between I also did badminton and dancing. I was very passionate about sports so my diary was almost full with only sports.
As I got older , I wanted to start with fitness.
It all started with my first workout with a personal coach. I found it so inspiring that someone motivated me and helped me to work out and eat healthy food.
I thought about all the sports and nutrition schedules I got. For example, I discovered that not only sport causes changes in the body, but what you eat is also so important. That's when I understood the meaning of "You are what you eat."
This is a quote from Nobel Coaching website:

Playing sports can have a positive impact on bone health as well. Engaging in a vigorous physical activity over a sustained period of time can improve bone mineral density, especially among girls.
Once I got used to my new lifestyle eating healthy food, I've unwittingly inspired other people as well. Trust me to become the best version of yourself.

My other hobbies besides sports

Besides sports, I love music. I started playing the piano when I started swimming.
So I've been doing it for as long as I've been doing sports.
I also played bass in orchestras. I've performed countless times in one of the oldest casinos in Beringen.
Besides playing the piano I also like to sing. Opera was really my thing!
That's how I first started in choir at school and then at the music school.
And last but not least, I've been playing the stage for several years now.
I've learned a lot. You'd wonder how I combined everything and my answer to that is:
if you can't make time, you don't like it enough!
