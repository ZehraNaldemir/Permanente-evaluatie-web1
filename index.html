Home

Chris Maurice Brown, better known as Chris Brown was born on May 5, 1989 in Tappahannock Virginia. He is an American RnB singer. Besides singing he is also a very talented dancer. How many men do you know who can do two things at the same time? That is what I thought! Besides his perfect voice he is also known for his dance talent. He grew up listening to his mother's soul records. She mainly listened to Michael Jackson, Sam Coocke, Usher, Stevie Wonder and Anita Baker. He himself preferred to go in the direction of rap. At the age of eleven he started singing himself. His father worked in a gas station, where one day by chance a music producer came to gas and told him that he was looking for new talent. Then his father said he had a son with talent. That is how Chris Brown's career started. With the help of his producer Scott Storch his first single was released: Run it!.

Personal life

Although he is known to many for his mistreatment with Rihanna, he broke out with his talent several times after that. At the age of 15 he signed a contract with a record company. After that he played several movies like Takers, This Christmas,... Now he has two children. He named an album to his first daughter "Royalty", also he wrote a song starring her in the videoclip. She is such a cute baby! Also not long ago he had a second child named "Aeko". So he is also a good parent. I have been a fan of him since I was 16 years old. My 16th birthday present was a concert ticket for the One Hell of a night Tour. Since then, my love for him has grown day by day. No matter what happens in his life, I will continue to support him as a loyal fan! Last but not least I would like to dwell for a moment on the song Deuces. This song has a special value for me. This is also the song he wrote when he was in jail for the mistreatment of Rihanna. Of course you can guess that the lyrics has a lot of meaning. It is mainly about his feelings and rejection of both Rihanna and the media. The blame was not entirely Chris Brown's. Rihanna had triggered it a bit herself. How do I know this? Years later he talked about this for the first time in his documentary Welcome to my life which is available on Netflix. It's a documentary about all the ups and downs in his life, Chris' whole life in a nutshell. I would strongly advise anyone to watch it who has negative recollections of Chris Brown because of Rihanna's negative influence on him in the media.
